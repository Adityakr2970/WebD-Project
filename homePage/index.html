"Atomic Habits" is a bestselling self-help book written by James Clear, first published in 2018. The book focuses on the idea that small changes, or "atomic habits," can lead to remarkable results over time. Clear emphasizes the power of habits in shaping our lives and provides practical strategies for building good habits and breaking bad ones."James Clear's 'Atomic Habits' is a game-changer in the realm of self-improvement literature. Clear's approach is refreshingly practical and evidence-based, making it accessible and actionable for readers of all backgrounds. By breaking down the complex psychology of habit formation into simple, actionable steps, Clear empowers readers to take control of their habits and, by extension, their lives.
